<xray-special-statement> :==

    selection=<atom-selection>
       Atom selection.
    to=<atomic-object>
       Destination for multiplicity. The multiplicity for the selected
       atoms is calculated and stored in <atomic-object>. Atoms at general
       positions are indicated by a 1, atoms at special positions are indicated
       by a multiplicity > 1, and unselected atoms are indicated by 0.
