restraints harmonic <restraints-harmonic-statement> end


<restraints-harmonic-statement> :==

    exponent=<integer>
       Exponent of the harmonic energy term. Default=2
    normal=<vector>
       Normal vector of a plane. To harmonically restrain the motion
       of an atom to this plane set the atom property harmonic energy
       constant to a negative value. To turn of the planar restraints
       set normal=(0,0,0).

       To setup the energy constants use the do statement.
