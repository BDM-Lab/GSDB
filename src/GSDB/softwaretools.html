Software tools

The goal of the GSDB is to create a comprehensive and common respository that contains 3D structures for Hi-C data from novel 3D structure prediction tools developed over the years. As a major contribution towards this goal, the members of the BDM Lab have published several research papers and made available several chromosome/genome 3D structure modeling tools to the research community. They are listed below.

  • 3DMax: [available on github]
  • LorDG : [available on github]
  • Chromosome3D. [available on github]
  • GMOL: [available on sourceforge]
  • Gen3D: [ available on sourceforge]
  • MOGEN: [available on github]
  • Hierarchical3DGenome: [available on github]
  • GenomeFlow: [available on github]
